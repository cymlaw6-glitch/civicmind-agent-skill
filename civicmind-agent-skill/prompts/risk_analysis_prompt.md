# Risk Analysis Prompt

Analyze the topic across these risk categories:

- policy risk;
- regulatory and compliance risk;
- market demand risk;
- competition risk;
- commercial model risk;
- funding and cash flow risk;
- operational risk;
- public opinion risk;
- data and privacy risk;
- low-probability high-impact risk.

For each risk, output:

- description;
- severity;
- probability;
- trigger condition;
- early warning signal;
- mitigation strategy.
