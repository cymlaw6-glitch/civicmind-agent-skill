# Company Intelligence Report: NovaAI Studio

## 1. Executive Summary

NovaAI Studio operates in a fast-growing but highly competitive AI content generation market. Its opportunity lies in vertical workflows for short drama creators rather than generic video generation. The key risks are content compliance, model cost, platform dependency and weak differentiation.

## 2. Business Model

Potential revenue models:

- subscription for creators;
- pay-per-generation credits;
- enterprise tools for MCN agencies;
- template marketplace;
- revenue share with distribution platforms.

## 3. Competitive Landscape

The company faces competition from general AI video tools, short drama production platforms, template editors and platform-native creation tools.

## 4. Risk Matrix

| Risk | Severity | Probability | Comment |
|---|---|---|---|
| Content compliance | High | High | Generated video may violate platform rules |
| High inference cost | High | Medium | Video generation is expensive |
| Product commoditization | Medium | High | Generic tools are easy to imitate |
| Platform dependency | Medium | Medium | Distribution rules may change |

## 5. Strategy Recommendations

Focus on workflow depth: script breakdown, shot list generation, character consistency, platform-safe prompt rewriting and batch production management. Avoid competing only on generic video quality.
